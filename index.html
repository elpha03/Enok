by ENOCK MAUNDU

Just a few days after Raila Odinga suffers a major setback after loosing in the A.U.C bid ,after 8 rounds,he merges with President Ruto to drive Kenya forward.The political re-union between President Wiliam Ruto and opposition chief Raila Odinga,leaves kenyan youth on their own."I thank my brother Raila Odinga for putting aside his personal aspirations and joining hands with the government to drive kemya forward." President Ruto's statement which was received differently by kenya citizens.

How can Ruto term the re-union helpful yet he kept complaining about former President Uhuru Kenyatta's handshake with Raila Odinga? Ruto complained that he was thrown out of government and Raila Amolo Odinga deputised former president Uhuru Kenyatta and they looted the treasury before he came in to find a already trembling economy with high cost of living.

Millions of kenyans are already alarmed by the act of the opposition leader joining hands with the goverment yet they are the goverment watchdog.Is Ruto trying to silence the opposition by pocketing their leader?This is yet to be known.However the political division among the opposition leaders already speaks alot about their power and ability to correct the goverment when it's heading to the wrong direction.

"Some leaders are driven by self interest and the greed for power and not concernced about the needs of the citizens " This is a rumour circulating all over terming the opposition leader as a self centered person. The political giant is yet to retire despite his old age and long time in his political career which at most times has proven to be in vain especialy his aspirations to be the presidents"Does he really mean well for kenyans?This remains a puzzle no are has been able to crack.
